About Us

ANI Auto Mobility Private Limited is a private limited company incorporated under the Companies Act, 2013, with its registered office in Karol Bagh, New Delhi (CIN: U45300DL2025PTC450426).

Founded in 2025, the company focuses on the design, development, manufacturing, branding, and nationwide distribution of premium automotive accessories and infotainment solutions. Under the visionary leadership of Mr. Aniket Kumar, ANI Auto Mobility is redefining the driving experience with innovation, safety, and style.

The company proudly operates proprietary brands such as Ultra Audio, Auto Nexa, Sound Auto, and High Beats, each known for technological excellence, durability, and customer satisfaction.
